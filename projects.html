Healthcare Modeling

Skin Cancer Prediction & Clinical Insight Modeling

Built logistic regression, random forest, and gradient boosting models to predict skin cancer risk from clinical and image features. The project focused on balancing predictive performance with interpretable clinical insight.

  • Engineered features and addressed class imbalance.
  • Validated models using an 80/20 split with cross-validation.
  • Evaluated ROC-AUC and precision/recall, then translated outputs into clinical risk scores.
